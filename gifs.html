Cozy GIFs from your favorite games

I miss MS Paint. The lack of transparency. The brutal three-undo limit.

My digital art career began at 10 years old, using sprite sheets to create my own Pokemon towns and posting them to forums.

This scene from left an imprint on my brain and I felt compelled to see it again.

If you have an 8/16/32-bit memory you would like reanimated as a GIF, send me a message and I will make your wildest dreams come true.

These reproductions are neither pixel nor frame perfect. Having watched a playthrough on YouTube, it turns out they're more like false memories. But this is how I remember them, and so I've stayed true to the composition and pacing of those memories.

My favorite detail: I couldn't find the original campfire animation, so I borrowed sprites from the back of Fire Kirby's head.
