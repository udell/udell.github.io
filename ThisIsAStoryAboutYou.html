This Is A Story About You

Three 8-year-old best friends time travel from 2003 to present day, uniting with two of their future selves on a journey from Philly to Pittsburgh in search of the last buddy.

This is A Story About You is an LGBTQIA+ sci-fi adventure about growing up, staying young, transitions, friendship, love, and the power of memory.
