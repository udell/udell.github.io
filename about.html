artist statement

Caroline [she/they] is an artist in working across sculpture, moving image, and print. Their practice considers issues of communication and the intricacies of human interaction - from linguistics to tactility to love. They are currently exploring the interface between people and materials with a focus in elevating the status of repurposed and sustainable materials.
