Baba

2015
video

Naomi is living with dementia. Though the disease has eliminated nearly all short term memory retention, she is one of the happiest people I know. She recognizes the impairment, but takes it in stride; her panacea being to laugh and say "I forgot that I forgot!" A Philly public school music teacher of 35 years, two aspects of herself she hasn't lost are an impeccable longterm memory and her musicality. In the course of one afternoon spent together, she twice told me the same frequently-repeated story from her college days. In both tellings, her cadence and mannerisms follow nearly the exact same patterns. It is a consistency in which I take comfort, knowing that she has these memories to provide inner company even as her capacity to process outer experiences changes.
